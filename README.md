# AjarDaya

**AI Decision Intelligence for Community Learning Enablement**

AjarDaya is a prototype built for the Gen AI Academy APAC Edition Cohort 2 Hackathon. It explores how AI can help schools, NGOs, mentors, and local communities turn fragmented learner and program signals into clearer, coordinated action.

The idea is rooted in Indonesia’s reality as an archipelago country, where learning support can be shaped by geography, connectivity, infrastructure, local mentor availability, and resource constraints, especially across remote and underserved communities.

## What AjarDaya Does

AjarDaya helps community learning stakeholders:

- Monitor regional learning signals through a Community Learning Command Center
- Identify learners who are on track, at risk, or behind through the Cohort Intelligence Analyzer
- Detect equity and support gaps through the Support Gap Lens
- Use Gemini-assisted reasoning to explain what a gap means, why it matters, and what actions to consider
- Simulate possible intervention options before committing resources
- Plan resource allocation across priority areas
- Generate a stakeholder-ready Community Action Brief with a 30-60-90 day roadmap
